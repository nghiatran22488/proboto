The Personal Story Behind Farm Stay USAA long time ago now we were romantically deluded urbanites. We wanted a simpler life so we bought a small sheep farm. The delusion ended pretty immediately! Within three years, after several attempts at livestock diversification that never could compete with the big guys, we decided to copy a model from Europe and opened a ‘farm stay’ in search of sustainability. Of course there were unintended benefits we never imagined: sharing our landscape and our hard-earned knowledge being foremost.
Our success became the impetus for Farm Stay USA. It was a way to pull similar operations together under one banner because, until 2010, there was no ‘farm stay’ travel niche in America. Farm stays as a form of rural vacation lodging began to rise to prominence as early as the 1980s in Europe. It was time to introduce travelers to American farmers, instead of sending them to Italy!
